GROWATT 3000-6000TL3-S

Growatt FB60 is designed for domestic PV system. It is installed between the PV output and main consumer unit. It has GFCI protection built in, and can measure as well as display the PV energy generated.

3 PHASE GRID-TIED INVERTER FOR RESIDENTIAL OR SMALL COMMERCIAL ROOFTOP APPLICATION. FANLESS DESIGN, COMPACT AND EASY FOR INSTALLATION.
